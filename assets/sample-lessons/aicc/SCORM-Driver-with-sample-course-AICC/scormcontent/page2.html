How to import and test courses in SCORM Cloud - Page 2

Now that your course is imported into SCORM Cloud, you need to launch your course and see how it looks.


1. Click on the "Library" button on the lefthand navigation of SCORM Cloud.


2. Click on the name of the course that you just uploaded.

3. Click on the "Launch" button for your course.

4. Your course should launch. If it doesn't, then there are SCORM or AICC errors inside your course.

5. After your course has launched, you can look at reporting for your course by clicking on the "Reportage" button.
