How to import and test courses in SCORM Cloud - Page 1

SCORM Cloud is a great place to test the SCORM or AICC conformance of your course.

You'll need a SCORM Cloud account. A trial account is available for free.


1. Go to cloud.scorm.com and signup for a free trial account.

2. Look for the "Choose File" button in the "Add Content" section on the right side of the SCORM Cloud homepage.

3. Locate your course, it should be a .ZIP file.

4. Select your course, and it will upload to SCORM Cloud. If your course has SCORM or AICC errors, it might not upload correctly.
